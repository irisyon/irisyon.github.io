Artist Statement

I'm a South Korean painter based in San Francisco.

I like trees — the way light passes through the gaps between branches, the way leaves filter what's seen past them, the way the roots give the rest of the tree the trust to sway and follow the wind.

I paint to become a little more like a tree.
